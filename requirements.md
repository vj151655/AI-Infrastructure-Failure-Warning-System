# Project Title
AI-Based Early Public Infrastructure Failure Warning System for India

## Problem Statement
India frequently faces infrastructure failures such as bridge collapses, road cave-ins, and structural damages due to aging structures and extreme weather conditions. Current systems are reactive and lack predictive monitoring.

## Objective
To design a scalable AI-driven early warning system that analyzes historical infrastructure failure data and environmental conditions to predict high-risk infrastructure zones before failure occurs.

## Functional Requirements
- Collect historical infrastructure failure data
- Integrate weather and rainfall datasets
- Perform risk pattern analysis using machine learning
- Generate risk scores
- Trigger early alerts for authorities
- Provide visualization dashboard

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Cloud scalability
- Secure data access
- High availability
- Modular design

## Stakeholders
- Government departments
- Disaster management authorities
- Public safety agencies

## Success Criteria
- Accurate risk identification
- Timely warning alerts
- Scalable architecture for nationwide deployment
